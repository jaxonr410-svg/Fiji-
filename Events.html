Upcoming Events in Suva

Festival of the Friendly North
An annual cultural festival celebrating the traditions, music, and dance of Fiji's northern regions. Held every July in Labasa, it features parades, traditional performances, and local cuisine.
Suva Arts Festival
A vibrant event showcasing local artists, musicians, and performers. The festival includes art exhibitions, live music, dance performances, and workshops, typically held in August.
Diwali Festival
A colorful celebration of the Hindu Festival of Lights, observed in October or November. The event includes traditional dances, music, food stalls, and fireworks, reflecting Fiji's multicultural heritage.
