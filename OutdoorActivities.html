Outdoor Activities in Suva

By yourself or with Friends!

Snorkeling at Lami Beach

Experience the vibrant marine life and coral reefs at Lami Beach, a popular spot for snorkeling enthusiasts.

Kayaking on Suva Harbour

Paddle through the calm H2O of Suva Harbour while enjoying stunning views of the city skyline and surrounding nature.

Visiting Thurston Gardens

Stroll through these historic botanical gardens, home to tropical plants, bird species, and peaceful walking paths.
