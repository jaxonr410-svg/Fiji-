Explore the Tropical Capital of Fiji

From colorful markets to scenic waterfronts and lush rainforests, Suva offers an unforgettable island experience. Dive into Fijian culture, savor tropical cuisine, and discover the heart of the Pacific Ocean.

Why Visit Suva?

  • Vibrant capital city located on the southeast coast of Viti Levu
  • Known for rich culture, local markets, and tropical scenery
  • Access to historical sites, museums, and waterfront parks
  • Delicious local cuisine and island adventures await
  • Perfect mix of urban energy and natural beauty
