Frequently Asked Questions

1. What’s the weather like in Suva?

Suva has warm tropical weather year-round with occasional rain showers.

2. What currency is used?

The Fijian Dollar (FJD). Credit/debit cards are widely accepted.

3. Is it safe?

Yes, but normal travel precautions should be observed.

4. How do I get around?

Buses, taxis, walking, and guided tours are common options.

5. Best time to visit?

May to October is generally preferred for cooler, drier weather.
